About Us

Empowering the Nation established itself in 2018, with greater
goals set to individuals. Our founder, motivated by her relatives
who never got the chance to obtain formal education wants to
give individuals the chance to change upskill themeselves.

By providing domestic workers and gardeners with additional
skills we want to be able for these indiciduals to become more
marketable when seeking employment and enable them to be paid a a higher rate.

Through either six-month courses who aims for Leadership
skills, or short six-week courses that are training programmes.

What we offer

Short Courses

Our short courses have the duration of six-weeks, the individual learns skills through the Short Skills Training Programmes that we offer.

Long Courses

Our long courses have a duration of twelve weeks (six-months), where individuals can learn additional leadership skills, these courses are extensive.
